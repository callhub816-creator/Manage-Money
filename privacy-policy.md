## 3. Cookies & Tracking

### Types of Cookies
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for site functionality (e.g., session cookies)
- **Analytics Cookies:** GA4 tracking (can be disabled via your browser settings)
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your choices (e.g., theme, language)

### How to Control Cookies
You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
